Share the PCB package(NGFF footpring) of M1n module: https://bbs.elecfans.com/jishu_2037182_1_1.html


It's very easy to use the NGFF footpring.

1.Add the .schdoc and .PCBdoc to your project.

2.Edit the .schdoc and update to .pcbdoc
3.Layout
4.Finish your work!

When using other boards, please pay attention to the position of the SMT nut.
If you want to buy the NGFF female socket,you can search the key word "NGFF B-KEY".

This connector is available in a variety of heights.
